About Strawnimals

Strawnimals is a fun hobby I started at the end of my senior year of high school. The colorful straws I got with my boba drinks always felt like such a waste to throw away, so I wanted to do something with them. Starting from making small animal designs from straw art books, I wanted to expand my creativity and began creating my own designs. Several of the boba strawnimal models on this shop are my own creations. The Strawnimal figures are all made PURELY with straws. After all, if glue or tape was used, it wouldn't be as interesting, right?

My Mission

I want to recycle straws and spread awareness of the environmental impacts that plastic straws have. Plastic straws poorly impact the environment due to their single use applications and plastic composition. They are not easily recyclable and can harm a lot of marine animals if they are ingested. After using my straws, I clean and wash them to turn into strawnimals as a means to recycle them and all proceeds made in the shop are donated to environmental groups that are actively solving this problem. While only a couple of straws may not have much of an effect in the long run, it's a start to at least spread awareness. Consider using reuseable straws as an alternative to drinks, so we can solve this problem together. The day I have no more straws to make Strawnimals, will certainly be a day to celebrate!
